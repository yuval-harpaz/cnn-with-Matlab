MEG-course

MEG course at Bar Ilan University, Israel. using mainly FieldTrip and SAM beamforming.

Our Magnetoencephalograph is 4D-Neuroimaging with 248 magnetometers. the course contains matlab scripts for analyzing MEG data. SAM beamforming is not matlab and the software is not free (obtained from NIMH). dependencies: ft_BIU cleanMEG_BIU SAM_BIU vsMovies AFNI matlab pack work-drafts

course1 Reading the data with pdf4D

course2 Methods for viewing the data, filters and baseline correction
